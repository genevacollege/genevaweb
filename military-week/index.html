Military Appreciation Week at Geneva College

Geneva College has a proud history of supporting our military and veteran community. That support continues today as Geneva is honored to serve military servicemembers, veterans, National Guard, reservists, and their dependents during Military Week.

We are excited to announce our second annual Military Ball, Veterans Breakfast, and Military Tailgate as part of our Military Week events.

The Geneva College Military Ball will observe the valued traditions of our branches, followed by keynote speaker,  LTC Tommy Richardson, Commander, Seal Team 10, traditional toasts and the ceremonial cutting of the cake. The evening will continue with music provided by locally favored DJ John Nerone.
